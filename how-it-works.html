How Punoshristi Works: Recycling Rewards in Bangladesh

Turn your empty bottles into rewards instantly with our AI-powered Reverse Vending Machines.

Three steps for you. A lifetime of impact for Bangladesh.

1

Find Machine & App

Locate the nearest Punoshristi Smart RVM using our mobile app. Whether you're on a university campus, a busy shopping mall, or a transit hub, an eco-friendly drop-off point is just around the corner.

smartphone Preview App Design open_in_new
2

Drop Bottle with AI

Simply insert your empty PET bottles or aluminum cans. Our state-of-the-art AI sensors instantly identify the material, size, and brand to ensure proper sorting and calculate your exact reward value.

  • check_circle Instant Material Recognition
  • check_circle Contamination Rejection
  • check_circle Fast Processing Speed
3

Scan QR & Earn

Once finished, the machine displays a unique QR code. Scan it with the Punoshristi app to instantly credit points to your digital wallet. Redeem these points for real-world value with our partners.

Digital Ad Funding

Our machines feature large, high-definition displays that serve dual purposes: guiding users and displaying dynamic advertisements. This innovative model helps subsidize the cost of recycling, making it accessible to more communities while providing brands with high-visibility, green-associated advertising space.

Partner Shop Benefits

Hosting a Punoshristi RVM drives eco-conscious foot traffic directly to your location. Retailers report increased sales as users redeem their recycling rewards in-store. It's a win for the environment, a win for the user, and a win for your business.
